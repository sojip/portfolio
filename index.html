About Me

I am a telecommunications engineer and a programming passionate . I am one of those who learned to code thanks to the internet. After obtaining my degree, I was employed as a network engineer for a period during which I had various responsibilities, including network architecture, monitoring and maintenance, firewall configuration, systems administration, and cyber security. This is when I fell in love with Python because of its extreme usefulness in cybersecurity, especially for malware analysis, vulnerability scanning, and penetration testing. After that, I decided to explore the language more deeply and took the CS50 online training on web programming with Python and Javascript. This course gave me a foundation in ,HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, JQuery, Bootstrap, Python, and FLask, and allowed me to get another job opportunity later as a software developer. During this time, I was working on projects related to the automation of tasks and the digitization of the company's customer journey, like the company's users web portal functionnalities. After finding myself to be a big fan of Javascript while working on a data visualization web application, I decided to follow The Odin Project's Full Stack Javascript path. This gave me the ability to work on full-stack web applications with the combination of ReactJs, Firebase, NodeJs, ExpressJs, and MongoDB. Javascript's ability to work on both the front end and the back end of a web application has made it my language of choice for a specialization in web development, while my always learning mindset is still ready to discover the unknown.
